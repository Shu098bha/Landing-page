INGREDIANTS
ANTIOXIDANT-RICH GREEN TEA EXTRACT

Packed with Antioxidant,green tea extract helps protect the skin from environmental stressors,reducing the appearance of fine lines and promoting a youthfull glow.

HYALURONIC ACID

Known for its ability to retain moisture, hyaluronic acid provides intense hydration, helping to plump the skin and reduce the signs of dehydration and aging.

VITAMIN-RICH JOJOBA OIL

Mimicking the skin's natural oils,jojoba oil helps balance oil production,providing moisture without clogging pores.It's also rich in vitamins and Antioxidants.

ROSEHIP SEED OIL

Packed with essential fatty acids and vitamins,rosehip seed oil promotes skin regeneration and can help to improve the appearance of scars and fine lines.

BOTANICAL EXTRACTS

Calming and soothing botanical extracts, such as chamomile and calendula,contribute to a gentle and nurturing formulation, ideal for sensitive skin types.
